Steps to follow:

1. Load index.html (We recommend CHROME for best performance)

2. Click on the F1 logo to start.

3. Use the time slider at the bottom to visualize different seasons(Click on the appropriate position on the slider track or drag the handle).

4. Choose a particular race to view by clicking on either the name of the race or its axis.

5. Hover on any Team or Driver path to emphasize its route.

6. Hover on any Driver path to see more details.

7. Click on the left or right button to view the next or previous race respectively.

8. Click on the slider handle to visualize the year again.

9. Click on the F1 logo at the bottom-left to go to the homepage.

10. Have fun! :)
